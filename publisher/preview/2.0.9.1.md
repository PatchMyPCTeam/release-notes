# Publisher Preview Version 2.0.9.1

**Release Date:** 2021-08-27

## Overview

This preview release improves conflicting process timeout options, increasing them to their respective platform maximums with appropriate buffers.

---

## Features

New capabilities and functionality added in this release.

- **Improved Timeout Options** - Improve conflicting process timeout options
  - ConfigMgr and Intune timeout increased to their respective maximums, minus a 15-minute buffer.
    - ConfigMgr App Max: 705 minutes
    - Intune Max: 45 minutes
